Who starts last



                         but shoots first



                                     does not miss



                                                 standing up



                                                             on the draw



                                                                         face to face



                                                                                     to kill him



                                                                                                 who moves first



                                                                                                             and thus betrays



                                                                                                                                     himself and is



                                                                                                                                                             to late



                                                                                                                                                             in his haste
